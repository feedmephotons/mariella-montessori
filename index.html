About Mariella Montessori

Rooted in Montessori, growing in Medford, NJ

Our Vision

At Mariella Montessori, we imagine a nurturing community where children, families, and educators grow side by side. Grounded in Dr. Maria Montessori's timeless principles, our learning environment is a place where curiosity is cherished, creativity is encouraged, and purposeful work fills each day with meaning. We integrate rich opportunities for creative exploration and guide the whole child in mind, hand, and heart. We believe learning is a shared journey, and by embracing parents and caregivers as partners, we support the growth of strong, thoughtful families who value lifelong learning. Through the interconnected environments of our Studio, Academia, and Collective, we strive to create a place where every person who walks through our doors feels inspired, empowered, and deeply connected.

Our Approach - Montessori in Practice

01

Child-Led Learning

We follow each child's natural curiosity and developmental pace, allowing them to explore subjects that captivate their interest.

02

Prepared Environment

Carefully curated spaces and materials encourage independence, concentration, and meaningful engagement.

03

Mixed-Age Groupings

Children learn from and with each other across age ranges, building leadership, empathy, and collaboration.

04

Hands-On Exploration

Sensorial materials and concrete experiences form the foundation for abstract thinking and deep understanding.

05

Independence & Confidence

Through self-directed activity, children develop problem-solving skills and belief in their capabilities.

06

Grace & Courtesy

Social skills and respect for others are woven naturally into daily interactions and learning experiences.

Why Choose Mariella

At Mariella Montessori, we honor the foundations of traditional Montessori education while thoughtfully responding to the needs of today's children and families. Our work is rooted in respect for each child's development, curiosity, and individuality.

We believe meaningful learning happens when academic depth, creative expression, and human connection are woven together. Children are given time, space, and guidance to engage deeply with their work, while families are welcomed as partners in a shared learning journey.

Mariella Montessori is more than a place to attend classes; it is a learning community where independence is nurtured, creativity is valued, and relationships matter. From early childhood through elementary years and beyond, we offer an environment where learning grows naturally and authentically.

In Loving Memory of Mariella

We named this space for Mariella, a dear friend and fellow Montessorian with whom the dream of building a Montessori school first took shape — a place where children could learn freely and feel deeply at home. Although she did not have the chance to see this dream realized, it lives on here today through the dedication and care of our growing community.

Mariella faced life's challenges with extraordinary courage, humor, and grace, meeting each day with a smile and an open heart. Inspired by her Peruvian heritage, the vibrant colors that fill this space reflect the warmth, joy, and richness she carried so naturally. They are woven into this community, shaping and inspiring us each day and all who gather here.

The flower is native to South America and is a symbol of lasting love, resilience, and the joy she brought into every life she touched.

Programs

Our Programs are divided into three environments

Studio

Studio offers hands-on creative experiences that invite exploration, expression, and joyful learning. Rooted in play, art, and imagination, these offerings support young children and caregivers as they create, discover, and connect together.

Walking - K

Creative Playtime

A Montessori-inspired art experience for children from walking age through Kindergarten, and their caregivers.

Each week, families explore open-ended activities, such as painting, building, printmaking, drawing, and stamping, through themes inspired by art history, nature, science, literature, music, and the elements of art. Guided by a Montessori Creative Guide, the program fosters independence, creativity, and joyful learning for children, while giving caregivers practical insights into Montessori principles.

Parent or caregiver participation is encouraged — optional if children are toilet independent.

Ages 2y - K

Creative Explorations

Creative Explorations is a Montessori-inspired art class where children explore materials, techniques, and the elements of art through process-based projects inspired by artists, nature, and culture.

Each class begins with a guided creative lesson followed by independent exploration in the studio.

Parent or caregiver participation is optional if children are toilet independent.

Ages 3y - K

Creative Storytime

Creative Storytime combines storytelling, art creation, and independent creative exploration in a joyful, Montessori-inspired environment.

Each session begins with a circletime story, inviting children to listen, imagine, and discuss the story's ideas, illustrations, and characters. The group then transitions into a guided, process-based art activity inspired by the story.

Creative Innovations

Creative Innovations is a Montessori-inspired art experience for children ages four and older that blends guided instruction with independent exploration in a beautifully prepared art environment.

Each class introduces new art tools, techniques, and materials, such as printmaking, papermaking, stencils, paper punches, rulers, and more, offering children opportunities to experiment, invent, and create.

Creative I-time

Creative "Independence" time gently introduces children to a school-like environment, supports those transitioning out of naps, and engages children who thrive through hands-on learning.
